About Me

MR. Vivek Ashok Todkari
Junior Engineer (E&TC)

Hello! I am Vivek Todkari from Pune. I would like to share my little journey of how I became freelancer photographer.
My journey started when I was studying at polytechnic of AISSMS, Pune. My dad gifted me a smartphone on my birthday which was just a month after joining the college. One of the key features of that smartphone was good camera. On freshers party I clicked many pictures of my friends and just after that got really Good feedback from them and my friends kept inspiring me to click some more photos. Which eventually made photography my favourite Hobby and I started focusing more on gaining more knowledge and acquire skills of photography. I got great support from my Family to persue my career in photography. My parents gifted me Canon 1500D camera on my birthday. In final year send off party at my college I made a video of my college life and got the chance to present it in front of our college on projector. I received great appreciation from my teachers and my fellow classmates for the video. After successfully completing my education I started working for a photo studio where I attended many events. I have now started my own adventure and working as a freelancer photographer. So I invite all of you to join me on my adventure with my buddy Canon 1500D with 18-55mm f / 3.5-5.6 | 55-250mm f / 4-5.6 | 50mm f / 1.8
